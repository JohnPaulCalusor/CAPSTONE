PAPSAS REGION 3

Clark-Pampanga – On June 26, 2025, the Philippine Association of Practitioners of Student Affairs and Services (PAPSAS) in Region 3 conducted its official oath-taking ceremony for the newly elected officers and members of the Board of Directors at Hotel Seoul, Clark-Pampanga. The occasion marked a remarkable milestone in the history of the regional chapter, as it reaffirmed its unwavering commitment to advance student welfare and promote quality services within the academic community. The ceremony stood not only as a formal recognition of new leadership but also as a symbol of renewed dedication to the organization’s long-standing mission and vision.

During the program, the incoming officers and directors solemnly pledged to uphold their duties with integrity and professionalism, ensuring that their actions remain aligned with the values of PAPSAS. Their commitment reflected the broader goals of the association: to continuously support student affairs practitioners, provide platforms for growth, and foster collaboration across institutions. The event also underscored the steadfast participation of the membership body, whose collective effort and dedication are essential in sustaining the chapter’s initiatives. By honoring its leaders and highlighting the spirit of service among its members, PAPSAS Region 3 reinforced its role as a vital force in strengthening student affairs and services across the region.
