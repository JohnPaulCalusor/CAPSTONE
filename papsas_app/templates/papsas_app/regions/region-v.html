The Region 5 Chapter of the Philippine Association of Practitioners of Student Affairs and Services, Inc. (PAPSAS) launched the Bicol Young Leaders Summit (BYLS) as its flagship student development program.

The 1st PAPSAS BYLS was hosted by the Ateneo de Naga University, Naga City, on January 20 – 21, 2023. More than 200 student leaders, student affairs and services (SAS) administrators and staff, and advisers of student councils and organizations from Bicol Region participated in this event.

The summit featured three resource speakers, namely: Ralph Jacinto Quiblat, director of the Ateneo de Manila University Office of Student Activities; Magno Edilberto Conag III, founder and CEO of Nueca Technologies, Inc.; and Ben Secretario, president of the Ten Outstanding Boy Scouts of the Philippines Association. Consistent with the BYLS’s whole-person formation framework, the said speakers discussed Self Awareness, Servant Leadership, Project Development, and Collaboration & Network Building.

Other highlights included The Amazing Race-inspired games, “BYLS Got Talent”, project development presentations, and a commitment-building pledge led by student leader Jerwin Roy Villacruz. 🎉
